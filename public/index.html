Key Formulas

1. Bulk Volume Calculation (Trapezoidal Method):
BVin²-ft = (h/2) × (A₀ + 2A₁ + 2A₂ + ... + 2Aₙ₋₁ + Aₙ)
2. Convert to Acre-ft:
BVacre-ft = BVin²-ft × Map_Scale² / 6,272,640
3. Original Oil In Place (OOIP):
N = 7758 × BVoil × φ × So / Boi / 1,000,000 (MMSTB)
4. Original Gas In Place (OGIP):
G = 43560 × BVgas × φ × Sg / Bgi / 1,000,000 (MMSCF)
Legend:
h = Contour Interval (ft)
A = Cross-sectional Area (in²)
Map_Scale = Map scale (1:X)
φ = Porosity (decimal)
Swi = Initial Water Saturation (decimal)
So = Oil Saturation (decimal) - fraction of pore space occupied by oil
Sg = Gas Saturation (decimal) - fraction of pore space occupied by gas in gas zone
Boi = Oil Formation Factor (bbl/STB)
Bgi = Gas Formation Factor (scf/cf)
BVoil = Bulk Volume of Oil Section
BVgas = Bulk Volume of Gas Section (if GOC present)
MMSTB = Million Stock Tank Barrels
MMSCF = Million Standard Cubic Feet
