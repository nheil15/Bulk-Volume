Key Formulas

Bulk Volume (BV) Calculation Methods:
1. Trapezoidal Method:
BV = (h/2) × (A₀ + 2A₁ + 2A₂ + ... + 2Aₙ₋₁ + Aₙ)
2. Pyramid Method:
BV = (h/3) × √(A₁ × A₂) for each interval
3. Simpson 3/8 Rule:
BV = (3h/8) × (A₀ + 3A₁ + 3A₂ + 2A₃ + ... + Aₙ)
OOIP (Original Oil In Place):
N = 7758 × BV × φ × (1-Swi) / Boi
Legend:
h = Contour Interval (ft)
A = Area at contour line (in²)
φ = Porosity (%)
Swi = Initial Water Saturation (%)
Boi = Oil Formation Factor (bbl/STB)
